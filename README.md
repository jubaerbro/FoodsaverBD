# FoodSaver BD

FoodSaver BD is a sustainability-focused web platform designed to reduce food waste in Bangladesh by connecting users with surplus food from restaurants, bakeries, and food businesses at discounted prices. The platform aims to make food more affordable for consumers while helping businesses reduce waste and contribute to a more sustainable community.

## Overview

Every day, a significant amount of edible food goes unsold and is eventually discarded. At the same time, many people look for affordable meal options. FoodSaver BD is built around the idea of solving both problems together by creating a digital platform where surplus but safe food can be offered at lower prices instead of going to waste.

This project is also part of my portfolio work, showing my interest in building practical, socially meaningful software products with modern web technologies.

## Features

- Browse discounted surplus food from local restaurants and bakeries
- Clean and modern landing page design
- Responsive user interface for desktop and mobile devices
- Structured sections for awareness, discovery, and engagement
- Scalable frontend architecture for future feature expansion
- Designed with sustainability and usability in mind

## Motivation

Food waste is a major challenge, and digital platforms can help reduce it by making surplus food visible and accessible. FoodSaver BD is motivated by the idea that technology can be used not only to build convenient products, but also to solve real community problems.

The main goals of the project are:

- Reduce unnecessary food waste
- Make surplus food more accessible and affordable
- Help restaurants and bakeries recover value from unsold food
- Promote sustainable and responsible consumption in Bangladesh
